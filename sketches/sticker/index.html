Blossom Branch, Degree Show Piece

Blossom Branch is an adapted project for the Digital Arts Computing Degree Show 2020. A mechanical sculpture made up of wire, 3D printing, an arduino, servo and fallen branches, completed in lockdown during COVID-19 pandemic.
